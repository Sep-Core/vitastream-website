Problem

In many homes and small public facilities, usable graywater from sinks is often discharged as waste. Meanwhile, water fees keep rising and sustainable usage becomes a real-world pressure—not just a slogan.

Without a practical, trustworthy reuse pipeline, daily water consumption stays high and the opportunity to save both water and cost is lost at the source.

Innovations

  1. Multi-sensor intelligent diversion system (pH 6.0–9.0, turbidity < 5 NTU; UV sterilizer + granular filter; follows standards to control solenoid valves and reduce misjudgment)
  2. Multi-pipe collection/processing system (two-pipe diversion; upper layer collects the clearest water; +30% extra reuse; +37% cleanliness)
  3. Self-power supply system (small hydro generator inside pipeline reduces external electricity use)
  4. App monitoring mode (real-time progress + visible water fee savings)
